About AM Equipamentos Médicos

Founded in 2010, AM Equipamentos Médicos has become a trusted name in the pre-owned medical equipment market. We specialize in sourcing, refurbishing, and distributing high-quality medical devices to healthcare facilities across Brazil.

Our team of biomedical engineers and technicians meticulously inspect and refurbish each piece of equipment to ensure it meets or exceeds original manufacturer specifications. We believe in making quality medical technology accessible to all healthcare providers.

With over a decade of experience, we've helped hundreds of clinics, hospitals, and private practices acquire the equipment they need at prices they can afford.
